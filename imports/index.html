I completed my Master's of Science in Software Engineering in March of 2019.
I feel technology and the many benefits and enhancements it provides should be accessible to all and I strongly support open-source software. All of my code is available through my github account here.
All my software is licensed under the GPL v3.
I am a full-stack web applications developer, currently leaning towards client-side and frontend development.
The Internet-of-Things is one of my greatest interests. Many of my projects are embedded software built with C++, FreeRTOS, and the libopencm3 libraries. The majority of the hardware I use utilizes ARM cortext microchips embedded in STMicroelectronics microcontrollers such as the STM32f1xx, STM32f3xx, STM32f4xx and STM32f7xx. Another MCU I favor is the Espressif line of chips, I have developed quite a few projects with the NodeMCU boards which use their esp8266 and esp32e chips.
